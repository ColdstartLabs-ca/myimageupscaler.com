## Executive Summary

### Massive growth in impressions, CTR is the bottleneck

Organic impressions **+207%** (28,986 -> 88,907) and clicks **+99%** (1,178 -> 2,342) vs previous period. Average position improved from 28.4 to 10.1. This is explosive growth driven by the blog expansion and pSEO pages.

However, **average CTR dropped 35%** (4.06% -> 2.63%) because most new impressions are in positions 4-15 where the snippet quality determines clicks. The CTR improvement plan (P1-P3) just completed addresses this for 43 blog posts, but 82 posts still have audit flags.

**Conversions remain at 0** across all organic pages — the biggest business risk. 2,129 organic sessions with zero conversions means the funnel is broken at the page level.
