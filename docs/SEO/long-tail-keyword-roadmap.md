# Long-Tail Keyword Roadmap for MyImageUpscaler

**Created:** 2026-02-25
**Strategy:** Target low-competition, long-tail keywords (500-5,000 searches/month) where DR 18 can realistically rank
**Goal:** 400-800 monthly organic clicks in 3-6 months

---

## Executive Summary

### The Problem
We're targeting high-volume keywords (500K+ searches) where competitors have 60-164x our authority. Result: 0 page 1 rankings, 68% of keywords rank 100+.

### The Solution
Pivot to long-tail keywords where our DR 18 can win. Focus on:
- **Platform-specific** (midjourney, stable diffusion, dalle)
- **Format-specific** (avif, heic, tiff)
- **Use-case-specific** (youtube thumbnails, etsy, real estate)
- **Scale-specific** (upscale 8x, 8k, 16x)

### Expected Outcome
| Timeline | Target | Est. Traffic |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| Current | DR 18, 0 page 1 rankings | 13 clicks/month |
| 3 months | 20 long-tail top 10 rankings | 200-400 clicks/month |
| 6 months | 50 long-tail wins, DR 25+ | 500-1,000 clicks/month |
