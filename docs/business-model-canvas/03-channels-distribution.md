### Freemium Model Design

#### Free Tier (The Hook)
**Monthly Limits:**
- 10 images per month
- Up to 2x upscaling
- Basic enhancement only
- No watermarks (differentiation!)
- Standard processing speed
- Email support only

**Strategic Purpose:**
- Demonstrate value immediately
- Create viral before/after shares
- Build email list for nurture
- SEO benefit (user-generated content)
- Word-of-mouth trigger

**User Experience:**
1. Land on site ‚Üí No signup required

4. Download without watermark

3. **Want faster processing:** A100 GPUs for Pro users
4. **Platform integration:** Shopify app requires paid plan
5. **Remove storage limits:** Free users lose images after 7 days

## Distribution Channel FAQs

**Q: Why so much SEO focus vs paid ads?**
A: SEO has 10:1 long-term ROI in this market, competitors prove it works, and high CPC makes paid expensive. SEO compounds, paid doesn't.

**Q: Won't freemium cannibalize paid plans?**
A: No‚Äîsuccessful SaaS like Let's Enhance use freemium. Free tier creates awareness and trust. Only 3-5% convert, which is expected and profitable.

**Q: Why Shopify before WooCommerce?**
A: Shopify merchants have higher income, better conversion rates, and app store provides built-in distribution. WooCommerce is phase 2.

**Q: How do we compete with free tools for SEO?**
A: We're not free‚Äîwe're "freemium professional." Target business users willing to pay for reliability, speed, and text preservation. Different buyer.

**Q: What if paid ads don't work?**
A: That's why we test small first. If CPA >$300 and LTV <$400, we cut budget and reallocate to SEO/PLG. Flexibility is key.
