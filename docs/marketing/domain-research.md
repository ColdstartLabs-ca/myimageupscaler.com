## Final Selection: `myimageupscaler.com`

### Rationale

1. **SEO Value (5/5)**
   - Exact match for "image upscaler" - the #1 keyword with 500K+ monthly searches
   - Strong long-tail coverage for "my image upscaler" queries

2. **Trust & Credibility**
   - .com is the most trusted TLD globally
   - No brand confusion risk

3. **Cost Efficiency**
   - ~$12/year registration vs $80-150+ for .ai domains
   - Lower ongoing costs

4. **Trade-offs Accepted**
   - 17 characters is longer than ideal
   - "my" prefix slightly less modern than alternatives
