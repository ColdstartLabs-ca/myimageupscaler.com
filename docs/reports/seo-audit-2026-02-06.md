**File**: `app/robots.ts`
- **Policy Change**: AI crawlers now ALLOWED for better AI search visibility
- GPTBot, ChatGPT-User, Google-Extended can now crawl content
- Enables inclusion in ChatGPT search, Google SGE, AI Overviews, Gemini

**Impact**:

- No more thin/generic pages indexed by search engines
- Better overall content quality
